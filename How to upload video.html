Steps to upload a video

HOME PAGE

Youtube Studio Link: YouTube Studio

Follow these steps:

  1. Click the link provided
  2. Once inside Youtube Studio, click on the create button on the top right corner
  3. next click on upload videos
  4. A screen will pop up, there you can either drag and drop a video from your file explorer or click on select files and then choose your video.
  5. Once your video has been selected, you will be taken to a page where you can customize the thumbnail, title and description of your video.
  6. Lastly, once you have customized your thumbnail and title, click on the next button 3 times where you will choose the option "public" and then you can hit publish.
  7. And thats it, you have succesfully uploaded a video to Youtube.
